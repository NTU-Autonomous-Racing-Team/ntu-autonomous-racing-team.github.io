1/10th the scale. 10 times the fun.

NTU Singapore Team racing in Jeju Island, South Korea

The NTU Autonomous Racing Team participates in F1Tenth competitions around the world, where we compete against teams of undergraduate and graduate students from top universities. Our car was built from the ground-up by NTU students according to the specifications laid out in the F1Tenth rule-book. Additionally, we have developed several autonomous racing algorithms. Currently, the team consists of students from MAE, SCSE, and IGP. If you are an NTU student and interested in joining us, please use this form (requires NTU email login).
